ADSB.lol is an Open Source ADSB aggregator.

Why?

Aviation intelligence firm JETNET has acquired ADS-B Exchange, a popular independent flight tracking platform. The platform sources its data from volunteers across the globe who set up receivers to receive real-time data from planes in mid-flight. This data is then fed into ADS-B Exchange's software, which compiles the inputs and displays a real-time map of all the world's in-transit flights.

ADSB.lol is an Open Source alternative to ADS-B Exchange. We are not affiliated with ADS-B Exchange. We welcome all feeders and developers to join us in our mission to provide a free, open source alternative to ADS-B Exchange.

To start feeding, see the adsb.lol feed client. The client feeds ADSB and MLAT data to adsb.lol and has checkboxes to feed also to adsb.one and adsb.fi, two community websites that have the same goal but are, unfortunately, not open source.
